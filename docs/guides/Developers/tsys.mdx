# TSYS Faucets

## Tanenbaum (NEVM) Testnet Chain

![img](https://miro.medium.com/max/1400/1*P1T6-9_mmIfGL7cFBBIR1A.png)

After connecting to the Syscoin Tanenbaum Testnet with MetaMask ([tutorial here](https://syscoin.medium.com/setting-up-metamask-for-syscoin-testnet-nevm-tanenbaum-e2bba17ff309)), head to https://faucet.tanenbaum.io/ to get some Syscoin.

- Copy your address from MetaMask



![img](https://miro.medium.com/max/700/1*NIOXTDyw-78PacWdPwz5pA.png)

- Click the the Tweet link on the faucet page.

![img](https://miro.medium.com/max/700/1*Tq4nUDa9-m3iloiqbdlbrQ.png)

This will open up Twitter with a Tweet.

![img](https://miro.medium.com/max/700/1*TwibQ5ZmeGcUaZo0kjZ3Dw.png)

- Next, paste in your address replacing the current address then tweet it.

![img](https://miro.medium.com/max/700/1*V3FvneJTOYv4CHroVFEYpg.png)

- Once tweeted, click share and copy the tweet link and head back to the faucet page.



![img](https://miro.medium.com/max/700/1*t5UTWYHmWaYLw_WCdK3gBg.png)

- Paste the link into the text box and click Give me SYS. Choose the amount of SYS you would like and wait one block to receive.

![img](https://miro.medium.com/max/700/1*8oAgaQXmAY9ojo3m8mB20Q.png)

Your MetaMask Wallet will now be funded with tSYS and you can now interact with dApps on Syscoin NEVM.
